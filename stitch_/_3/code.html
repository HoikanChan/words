Ephemeral

/əˈfem(ə)rəl/

Mastered
Adjective Formal

The Decree

Lasting for a very short time; transient. Originating from the fleeting nature of existence, often used to describe beauty or moments.

“

The autumnal colors are beautiful but ephemeral, lasting only a few weeks before the leaves fall.

“

Fashion trends are notoriously ephemeral, changing with the whims of the season.

Morphology

Epi + Hemera

From Greek: 'Epi' (upon) + 'Hemera' (day). Literally meaning: 'Lasting only a day'.
